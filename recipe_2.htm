Yum Yum Kitchen Fresh Food Done Right!

Ingredients

Level:Easy Serves:4 Total:5min

  • 1/2 cup Vanilla Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1 cup berries

Directions

  1. Spoon half of the yogurt into a see through glass.
  2. Top with half of the granola and then half of the berries. Repeat with remaining yogurt, granola, and berries. Enjoy!

  • Cholesterol 5 mg
